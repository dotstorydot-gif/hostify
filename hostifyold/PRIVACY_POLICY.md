# Privacy Policy for .Hostify

**Last Updated: January 2026**

.Hostify ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Profile Information**: Name, email, phone number, and nationality.
- **Identity Verification**: We collect images of ID documents to verify your identity before allowing bookings.
- **Booking Data**: Property selection, check-in/out dates, and guest count.
- **Device Information**: Basic technical data required for app functionality and notifications.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To facilitate property bookings and guest services.
- To verify your identity for safety and security.
- To send important notifications regarding your stays.
- To provide customer support.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. Your information is shared only with:
- **Supabase**: Our backend service provider for data storage.
- **Property Owners/Landlords**: Only necessary booking info (name, dates) is shared to facilitate your stay.

## 4. Your Rights
You can request to delete your account and personal data at any time via the App Settings or by contacting support at info@dot-story.com.

## 5. Security
We use industry-standard encryption (via Supabase) to protect your sensitive documents and personal data.
